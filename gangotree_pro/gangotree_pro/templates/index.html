About Us

Our story, mission, and vision

Genesis of Gangotree

GANGOTREE SOCIAL ORGANISATION is a non-governmental social development organization established by a group of dedicated development activists. Their aim is to extend the benefits of development to the needy, particularly focusing on tribal communities. The organization has successfully operated in this field, addressing various aspects of welfare and development, and has gained recognition for its efforts. It is registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 as a non-profit, non-political entity.

There is a strong sense of commitment, dedication, and enthusiasm among the members, who are eager to provide assistance to those in need. GANGOTREE SOCIAL ORGANISATION has conducted various programs, actively involving the local community, with a special emphasis on tribal areas and individuals facing challenges. The organization has well-defined Bye-laws and a Memorandum of Association, along with a General Body and Governing Body, to enhance transparency and functionality.

Since its inception, the Organization has effectively executed diverse activities in line with its mission: "To enhance the quality of life by intervening in areas related to health and livelihood, with a special focus on empowering women and ensuring proper child care in the community."

The registered headquarters is located at Plot No. 156/2794, At/Po—Sisupalgarh, Gyatri Vihar, Old-Town, Bhubaneswar, in the district of Khurda. Additionally, there are field offices at Pritipur—Alakunda, District-Jajpur, and in the K.B.K. district.

Gangotree Social Organisation operates in both coastal and tribal areas of Odisha, working closely with Dalits and other socially and economically disadvantaged groups.

Our Vision

To achieve sustainable development and establish a new socio-economic order where the poor and women have equal opportunities, guided by the values of sustainable development, global common good, and compassion for the weaker sections of society.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life by intervening in areas related to health and livelihood, with a special focus on empowering women and ensuring proper child care in the community. This is aimed at achieving sustainable development and establishing a new socio-economic order where the poor and women have equal opportunities.

Our Objective

  • To work for the welfare and development of marginalized tribal communities in Odisha
  • To empower women and ensure their equal participation in socio-economic development
  • To address critical issues such as poverty, environmental development, and reproductive and child health
  • To integrate target communities into the mainstream development process
  • To ensure community participation and safeguard their rights and entitlements
  • To collaborate with partners and government bodies for sustainable impact
